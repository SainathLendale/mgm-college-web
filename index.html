Welcome To MGM

Mahatma Gandhi Mission Trust was established on December 20, 1982, in Nanded, Maharashtra, with a futuristic vision
to provide qualitative education and health services in the underdeveloped Marathawada region. Since inception, the trust
has focused on providing healthcare services, school education, and higher education with dedication and commitment.
In the course of time, MGM extended its services to Aurangabad, Navi Mumbai, and Parbhani in Maharashtra, and Noida
in Uttar Pradesh. A chain of schools and colleges in the faculties of engineering, architecture, medical, nursing,
management, computer science and IT, biotechnology, bioinformatics, fine arts, and journalism stand testimony to the
endeavors of the trust. Mahatma Gandhi Mission has been instrumental in promoting classical dance and music for art
seekers. The Trust has also paid heed to the aspect of physical fitness by promoting sports activities and creating the
facilities for them. Services to society at the grass-roots level have been the basic vocation of the trust, along with
education. The Trust has been instrumental in providing prompt and efficient healthcare services to the economically
weaker section of a society. The Trust's hospitals and medical colleges underscore its commitment to human resource
development, social health, and welfare.
